Trip #6 – Waterfront Chill, OH

2024-08-09

Trip #6 to Ohio was a blast! Perfect weather (none of last trip’s sauna heat) and a brand-new campsite right on the water.

Xander managed a “zorched” foil dinner and later ignited an Oreo over the fire—chef-kiss chaos. Dawson out-did himself with camp-barista coffee, Khai mastered a new card game, and Rory won MVP wood-collector.

The kids all crowned it “best trip yet,” but it flew by. Already counting down to a fall-colors edition full of fresh laughs.

Nerd note: The Fusion just crossed 10 k miles hauling us back and forth to Ohio once a month since April.

These kids are amazing, and I wish I had more time with them.
