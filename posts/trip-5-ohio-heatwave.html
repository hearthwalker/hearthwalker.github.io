Trip #5 – Ohio Heatwave & AC Hacks

2024-07-12

Trip #5 ended way too quickly! Left Albion on Thursday night, cruised through a blissfully calm Midwest night, and rolled into our familiar Ohio campsite by noon Friday. Same spot, new memories—one of those blink-and-you-miss-it weekends.

Xander discovered dry-shampoo sorcery (good-bye greasy hair), Dawson split oak like a lumberjack and leveled up his espresso game, Khai talked me into trendy PRIME drinks (“Dad, they’re bussin’”), and Rory tore around the loop with new campground friends.

The weather? Sauna-level sticky. Salvation came from Austin’s portable AC + Scott’s spare batteries—kept the tent chill deep into the night. Heroes.

I already miss the chaos and can’t wait to brave the Ohio heat again for Trip #6 in August. Rumor says a kiddie pool full of ice might make the packing list.

#StatsForNerds: The Fusion averaged 36.0 mpg over 1,600 miles—barely a couple gallons more than one full tank in the old Excursion. I’ll take that win.
