Trip #8 – Independence Dam SP, OH

2025-05-16

Ohio Trip #8 to Independence Dam State Park is in the books—equal parts adventure and heart-warming dad-duty.

I clocked 1,600 miles in the new-to-me 2018 Grand Caravan—28 hours of windshield time that my bones protested but my heart signed off on the moment those kids smiled.

We geared up for rain that never came—storms split around us and left dry, fairy-tale camping weather. Xander & Dawson shredded logs with wild axe enthusiasm; Rory & Khai ruled hammock acrobatics (and squabbles). A cheeky raccoon stole our trash for comedic effect.

Massive thanks to Joel (gas + groceries), Austin (tent AC/heater), the Applied crew (snacks!), and Scott (power stations). Your kindness turned a good trip legendary.

But the real gold lies in the quiet dawns, the lantern-lit giggles, the sleepy yawns at sunrise—tiny treasures that make every mile worthwhile.
